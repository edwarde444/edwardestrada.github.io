Cardinals win big

The Saint Louis Cardinals won the biggest game of their season on Sunday when they beat the New York Mets at Citi Field in New York. The game started off poorly and the Cardinals looked sure to lose being down 5-1 halway through the first innings. The game completely changed when Pete Grossman got 3 homeruns, a personal record. Star pitcher David Rosswell also had a great game pitching a 23. After the game, Rosswell told the Tactical Times that he was pumped up for the game given the rivalry between the two teams and wants to continue his great form into the next games. The Cardinals play the Dodgers on the 4th.
