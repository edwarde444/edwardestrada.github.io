Tottenham Hotspurs vs Bayern Munich Preview

The Tottenham Hotspurs will play Bayern Munich in the UEFA Champions League on Sunday. Bayern Munich are the reigning champions of the competition. Last time the two teams played each other, Tottenham won 3-1 however Bayern were missing out on star striker Robert Lewandowski who had a calf injury. Lewandowski will be playing this match. A key matchup this game will be Tottenham striker Harry Kane vs. Bayern defender Jerome Boateng. The two previously played together at Tottenham before Boateng was bought by Bayern for 40 million British pounds.
