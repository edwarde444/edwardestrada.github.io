Pioneers win by 10

The Michigan Pioneers beat the Gonzaga Bulldogs 85-75 to progress to the final four of the NCAA championship. Many considered the Pioneers the underdogs but great performances from Pete Drull who got a triple double with 28 points and Mike Smith who got 10 blocks, 10 rebounds and 8 points guided the Pioneers to their first ever final four. The coach Ron Smith said he couldn't wait to play in the final four and that this game would go down in history. The Pioneers will play the winner of the North Carolina Tarheels vs Duke Blue Devils game to be played on Sunday next week.
